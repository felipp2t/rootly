---
description: Asks clarifying questions before answering to better understand your needs
allowed-tools: Bash
---

When the user asks a question or describes a topic, do NOT answer it directly yet.

Instead, analyze the question and identify what additional context would help you give a significantly better, more tailored answer. Then ask 3–5 targeted clarifying questions that would most change or improve your response.

Guidelines:
- Ask only questions that genuinely affect the quality of your answer
- Keep questions concise and easy to answer
- Group related questions if possible
- After the user answers, THEN provide a thorough, context-aware response

Start by briefly acknowledging what the user is asking, then present your questions clearly numbered.
